Digital Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP)

Data standard and digital requirements for education, health and care (EHC) plans

Local authorities must comply with the DfE’s minimum digital requirements and data standard for SEN2 reporting and EHC plan transfer purposes.

This manual is for:

  • local authority, health, and education SEND leads
  • local authority IT and digital leads
  • data controllers and responsible officers
  • suppliers of software for EHCP or information management

You can use this manual to assess you current EHCP digital systems and data against our minimum requirements. You can then use the guidance to assess how best to approach improving your system based on your local authority's needs. You can also contribute to the requirements and data standard through the community and find out what other local authorities are doing.
