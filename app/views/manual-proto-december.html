Data standard and digital requirements for education, health and care (EHC) plans

From: Department for Education.

  1. Contents

Local authorities must comply with the DfE’s minimum digital requirements and data standard for SEN2 reporting and EHC plan transfer purposes.

This manual is for:

  • local authority, health, and education SEND leads
  • local authority IT and digital leads
  • data controllers and responsible officers
  • suppliers of software for EHCP or information management

You can use this manual to assess you current EHCP digital systems and data against our minimum requirements. You can then use the guidance to assess how best to approach improving your system based on your local authority's needs. You can also contribute to the requirements and data standard through the community and find out what other local authorities are doing.

  1. Assess your local authority’s current compliance

Use our assessment tool to find out how you current EHCP service meets with our digital system requirements and data standard.

  1. Digitising EHCP systems

This guidance is for local authorities looking to implement or improve their digital EHCP systems to meet the standards and requirements.

  1. Data standard technical guide

This guidance is to help local authorities capture the data required for processing EHCPs.

  1. Digital systems requirements technical guide

These requirements are for local authorities updating or implementing a digital EHCP system.

  1. Use our transfer tool

Once you are compliant, you can use our transfer tool to send an individual child or young person’s EHC plan and associated data to another local authority.

  1. Join the community

We have a private slack channel you can join to provide feedback, ask for and offer advice and share your progress.
